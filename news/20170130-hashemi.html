News»Departure of Behnam ("Chebfun3") Hashemi

30 January 2017

Tonight the Chebfun team gathered to say au revoir to Behnam Hashemi, who is taking up a faculty position in the Department of Mathematics at the Shiraz University of Technology. Behnam wrote Chebfun3 single-handedly, about 10 thousand lines of code, and we will miss him!

From left to right: Mohsin, Sarah, and Amal Javed, Silviu Filip, Behnam Hashemi, Hadrien Montanelli, Yuji and Satoko Nakatsukasa, Ásgeir Birkisson, Nick Trefethen. Photo by Kate McLoughlin.
