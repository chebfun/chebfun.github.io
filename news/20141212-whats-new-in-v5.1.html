News»What's new in Chebfun Version 5.1

12 December 2014

We are pleased to announce the release of Chebfun Version 5.1 for numerical computing with 1D and 2D functions. The main new features are:

  • Fast solution of ODE initial-value problems via "\" (see Chebfun Guide, Chapter 10)
  • Updated CHEBGUI interface for ODE, PDE, and eigenvalues
  • New "trig" capabilities for periodic functions and ODEs (see Chebfun Guide, Chapter 11)
  • New function collections gallery, gallerytrig, gallery2 (see Chebfun Guide, Section 1.8)

Also note these new developments:

  • Rewritten Chapter 10 of the Chebfun Guide discusses ODE IVPs
  • New Chapter 11 of the Chebfun Guide for periodic functions
  • Chebfun now has an active wiki on its GitHub page
  • Chebfun User's Day will take place on 17 March at Oxford

There are a number of new Examples in the collection which demonstrate the use of new features. Among them are the following:

  • Galleries: Gallery and Gallerytrig
  • IVPs: Pythagorean planets · Modelling infectious disease outbreaks
  • Periodic functions: Fourier spectral collocation · Nonlinear periodic ODE · A periodic ODE system · Time-dependent PDEs on a periodic interval with EXPM · Periodic ODE eigenvalue problems
