To cite Chebfun in a publication, we recommend the following:

T. A. Driscoll, N. Hale, and L. N. Trefethen, editors, Chebfun Guide, Pafnuty Publications, Oxford, 2014.

Below is a list of Chebfun-related papers written by members of the Chebfun Team. For convenience, a BibTeX file is available. There are also many Chebfun-related publications by others. If you use Chebfun in a book, paper, website, or other project, we'd love to hear about it!
